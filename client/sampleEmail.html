Dear UCLA Student

We are writing to you due to a recent security breach affecting UCLA student accounts. It's crucial that you immediately enable two-factor authentication (2FA) to significantly enhance the security of your account.

This breach highlights the importance of layered security. 2FA adds an extra layer of protection, making it much harder for unauthorized individuals to access your information, even if their password is compromised.

If you have any questions, please contact the UCLA Information Technology Help Center at https://ithelp.ucla.edu/.
